PDF to PPTX Reference Placement

How to run:
1. If you received a `.dmg`, open it and drag `PDFtoPPTXReference.app` into `Applications`
2. If you received a `.zip`, extract it to a normal folder
3. Open `PDFtoPPTXReference.app`
4. If macOS warns about security settings, right-click the app and choose `Open`, or allow it in `System Settings > Privacy & Security`
5. Wait for your browser to open automatically
6. Drop one PDF and one PPTX into the upload box
7. Download the updated PPTX when processing finishes

What you need installed:
- LibreOffice, or
- Microsoft PowerPoint

Notes:
- LibreOffice is used first when available
- The app runs locally on your machine and opens in your browser
