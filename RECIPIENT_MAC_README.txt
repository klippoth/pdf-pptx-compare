PDF to PPTX Reference Placement

Installation and first launch:
1. If you received a `.dmg`, open it and drag `PDFtoPPTXReference.app` into `Applications`
2. If you received a `.zip`, extract it to a normal folder and move `PDFtoPPTXReference.app` into `Applications`
3. Open the app once from `Applications`
4. If macOS blocks it, go to `System Settings > Privacy & Security`
5. In the Security section, allow `PDFtoPPTXReference.app` anyway
6. Open the app again from `Applications`
7. If prompted again, confirm with `Open`
8. Wait for your browser to open automatically

How to use:
1. Drop one PDF and one PPTX into the upload box
2. When processing finishes, the updated PPTX is placed into your `Downloads` folder and opened automatically

What you need installed:
- LibreOffice, or
- Microsoft PowerPoint

Notes:
- LibreOffice is used first when available
- If LibreOffice is missing but Microsoft PowerPoint is installed, the app will use PowerPoint automatically
- The app runs locally on your machine and opens in your browser
- This no-AI build does not require any PowerPoint add-ins
- Because the app is not notarized, the first launch may require approval in `System Settings > Privacy & Security`
